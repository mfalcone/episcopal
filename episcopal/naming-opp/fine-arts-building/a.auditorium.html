Proscenium Theater/Auditorium (Reserved)

The 600-seat auditorium will be the main performance venue in the new Performing Arts and Assembly Center. Equipped with a state-of-the-art digital control center, orchestra- and balcony -level seating, and a proscenium stage, the auditorium will offer a premium space for student performances, guest speakers and divisional assemblies. The auditorium will serve as the premier gathering place for all prominent events on campus.
